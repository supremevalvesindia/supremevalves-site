Industrial Valves

Globe Valve

Linear-motion valve for throttling and flow control; available in 2-way and 3-way, manual or actuated.

Gate Valve

Full open/close isolation with minimal pressure drop; ideal for on/off services.

Ball Valve

Quarter-turn shutoff with tight sealing; floating and trunnion options.

Butterfly Valve

Lightweight quarter-turn design for large diameters and low pressure drop.

Non-Slam Check Valve

Spring-assisted disc closes quickly to prevent reverse flow and water hammer.

Strainer (Y / Tee)

Protects equipment by removing debris; SS904L 20-mesh available.

Control Valve (Actuated)

Automated throttling using electric/pneumatic actuators and positioners.

Pressure Safety Valve (PSV)

Opens at set pressure to protect equipment from overpressure.

NRV / Swing Check

Simple non-return design that prevents backflow.

Diaphragm Valve

Corrosion-resistant; suitable for slurries and hygienic services.

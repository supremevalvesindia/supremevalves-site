Ball Valves — Overview

Floating and trunnion designs with soft or metal seats. Available in 1pc, 2pc, and 3pc constructions, with fire‑safe options.

Types

  • Floating — 1pc/2pc/3pc
  • Trunnion — 2pc/3pc
  • Soft seat (PTFE/RPTFE/Devlon) and metal‑seated
  • Fire‑safe to API 607 (as applicable)

Classes & Ratings

ASME Class 150–1500; PN16–PN100 (as applicable). Temperature according to seat & body ratings.

Material Combinations

  • WCB/CF8/CF8M/CF3M
  • Duplex/Super Duplex (on request)

Applications

Oil & gas, chemicals, utilities, steam (with suitable seats), water, hydrocarbons.

Automation Compatibility

  • Electric actuators — on/off and modulating (4–20 mA / 0–10 V)
  • Pneumatic actuators — double‑acting / spring‑return with accessories

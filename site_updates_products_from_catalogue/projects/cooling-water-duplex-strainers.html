Cooling Water — Duplex Strainers Retrofit

Implemented twin basket duplex strainers with changeover to enable uninterrupted filtration during maintenance, improving availability of the cooling water system.

Scope

  • Design and supply of duplex strainers with SS316 baskets, DP taps and blow‑down.
  • Flanged Class 150 construction with quick‑opening covers.
  • On‑site commissioning assistance and training.

Results

  • Zero unplanned shutdowns for basket cleaning.
  • Lower average differential pressure across filtration.
  • Improved pump protection and longer service life.

Valves Used

  • Ball (floating/trunnion)
  • Gate
  • Globe control (modulating)
  • Check (dual‑plate, non‑slam)
  • Strainers

Material Selection

CF8M/316L for corrosive services; Duplex/Super Duplex for chlorides; WCB/LCB for utilities.

P/T Considerations

Class 150–1500 typical; temperature as per process media and seat selection.

Failures & Mitigations

  • Seat erosion → strainers and filtration
  • Galling on stainless fasteners → proper lubricants and materials
  • Thermal expansion issues → flexible connections/expansion loops

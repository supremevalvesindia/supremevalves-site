Oil & Gas

Valves Used

  • Ball (floating/trunnion)
  • Gate
  • Globe control
  • Check (swing/dual‑plate/non‑slam)
  • Strainers (Y/Basket)

Material Selection

  • WCB/LCB for carbon services; CF8M/316 for corrosive
  • Duplex/Super Duplex for seawater/brine

Pressure/Temperature

ASME Class 150–1500 typical; temperature per media and seat selection.

Typical Failures & Mitigations

  • Seat damage due to debris → upstream strainers, blowdown
  • Stem packing leaks → graphite packing, proper torque
  • Water hammer → non‑slam checks, slow actuation

About Supreme Valves

Supreme Valves is a sister concern of Superb Industrial Corporation (established in 1977 in Ahmedabad). The founding family has a combined 100+ years of experience in valves. We manufacture and export manual and automated valves, leveraging Ahmedabad’s strong ecosystem of foundries, manufacturing units, and skilled suppliers.

What We Do

  • Engineering‑grade product selection
  • Manual and automated valve packages (electric/pneumatic)
  • Documentation, inspection, and export packing

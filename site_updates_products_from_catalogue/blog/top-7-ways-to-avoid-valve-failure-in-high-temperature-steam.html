Top 7 Ways to Avoid Valve Failure in High‑Temperature Steam

  1. Select high‑temperature materials (e.g., WC6/WC9, SS316/347 for trims).
  2. Use graphite packing and spiral wound gaskets rated for temperature/pressure.
  3. Ensure proper pressure class and PT ratings with safety margin.
  4. Use hardfaced trims (Stellite) for erosion/cavitation resistance where applicable.
  5. Install proper supports, expansion loops, and insulation to manage thermal growth.
  6. Provide strainers upstream of control/PRV to protect against debris.
  7. Commission with gradual warm‑up and verify leak tests per API 598.

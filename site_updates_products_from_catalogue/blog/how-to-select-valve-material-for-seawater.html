How to Select Valve Material for Seawater

Seawater is highly corrosive due to chlorides, oxygen, and biofouling. Select alloys with proven resistance and consider design details that avoid crevices and stagnant zones.

Recommended Materials

  • SS316/316L: acceptable for splash/short exposure; risk of pitting/crevice in warm waters.
  • SS904L / Super austenitic: improved pitting resistance.
  • Duplex/Super duplex: strong chloride resistance and strength (validate PREN).
  • Cu‑Ni (90/10, 70/30): excellent for seawater systems and condensers.
  • Coatings: epoxy/ceramic internal coatings with proper QA.

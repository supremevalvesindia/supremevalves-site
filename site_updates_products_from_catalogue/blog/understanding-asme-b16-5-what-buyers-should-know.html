ASME B16.5 — What Buyers Should Know

ASME B16.5 covers pipe flanges and flanged fittings from NPS 1/2 through NPS 24. For valves, this determines the flange facing, dimensions, and pressure classes compatible with mating flanges.

Key Points

  • Pressure classes: 150, 300, 600, 900, 1500 (pressure–temperature dependent).
  • Facing types: RF, RTJ; ensure matching serration and gasket selection.
  • Dimensions: follow B16.5 for OD, bolt circle, drillings; verify material group numbers.

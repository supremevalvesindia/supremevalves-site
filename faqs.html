FAQs

Answers to common questions
What is the difference between Gate and Globe Valves?
Gate valves are isolation valves for full open/close service, while globe valves are designed for throttling and precise flow control.
Do you supply IBR-certified valves?
Yes. We supply IBR Globe and Gate Valves for steam and high-temperature services.
Can you provide valves with third-party inspection (TPI)?
Yes. We can arrange TPI with BV, TUV, or Lloyd’s as required by the project.
Can valves be supplied with actuators?
Yes. Electric or pneumatic actuators with positioners can be supplied for globe/ball/control valves.
Which materials are recommended for corrosive service?
316L and 904L stainless steels, Duplex, or Alloy Steel depending on the medium and temperature.
Do you export globally?
Yes. We export to the Middle East and other international clients; export documentation and packaging are supported.

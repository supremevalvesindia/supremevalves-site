Duplex Strainer — Working Principle

Duplex strainer changeover: one basket online while the other is cleaned.

A duplex strainer consists of two baskets connected via a common changeover valve. Operators switch flow from a fouled basket to a clean one without stopping the pump, enabling continuous operation and protecting downstream equipment.

Flow Path Diagram

Cleaning Steps

  1. Switch the changeover valve to route flow to the clean basket.
  2. Isolate and depressurize the fouled basket; open cover and remove debris.
  3. Reinstall basket, re-pressurize, and switch back when needed.

Advantages vs Y-type and Basket Strainers

  • No shutdown for cleaning; improved uptime.
  • Lower risk of bypassing debris during maintenance.
  • Better suited for high solids or critical services.

Typical Applications

  • Seawater cooling, phosphoric acid, slurry lines, utilities.
